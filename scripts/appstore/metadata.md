## Description  (4000 chars max)

```
SN AXIS is the official remote for the AXIS Smart Knob Shifter
— a precision, IMU-driven gear shifter built by SN Motorsports for
simulators, custom builds, and tactile-control enthusiasts.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Pair your AXIS knob in seconds over Bluetooth.
• Tune shift behaviour live: dwell time, dead-band, hysteresis, lock
  thresholds, low-pass smoothing — every knob has a 1-line plain-
  language explanation so you know what each control does.
• Calibrate the four shift modes: PRND, sequential, H-pattern (4/5/6
  speed), and AUTO. Step-by-step wizard with stillness gating and a
  re-zero on entry so your captures land clean.
• Customise the look. Brand it: device name, accent colour, foreground
  and muted colour slots, per-element colours for gear digit, meter
  ring, and labels. Pick your transition style and gear digit
  animation.
• Set a screensaver. Upload an image, GIF, or short video and the app
  converts it to the AXIS native 128-colour packed format in your
  browser — the device pulls it over Bluetooth and shows it when
  idle. Live preview before you save.
• Push firmware updates over the air. The OTA page reads the manifest
  from a public GitHub Pages URL and streams the new image to the knob
  over WiFi; a USB-rescue merged.bin is bundled alongside every
  release in case anything goes sideways.
• Tune the live G-meter, run a 0-60 launch timer, and view the live
  gear-pattern visualisation.
• Music mode (v2.6+): play / pause / next / previous / volume up /
  down right from the knob, with live track title, artist, position
  bar, and best-effort album art pulled from MusicBrainz on WiFi.

WHO IT'S FOR

Sim-racers who want a real-feel manual or sequential shifter, custom-
build enthusiasts who want their interior to look bespoke, and anyone
who wants a precise tactile control surface on their desk.

PRIVACY FIRST

No accounts. No analytics. No third-party SDKs. The app talks only to
your own AXIS device over Bluetooth, with one anonymous network
request to GitHub Pages for firmware update checks. Full policy at
snmotorsport01.github.io/axis-companion/privacy.html.

NEED AN AXIS KNOB?

The companion app is free. The AXIS Smart Knob Shifter hardware is
available from SN Motorsports. Tap DEMO MODE on the first screen to
explore the app without a device — every page shows representative
data so you can see exactly what the experience looks like.

Compatible with iPhone running iOS 14+. iPad supported (works on any
iPad with Bluetooth LE).
```
